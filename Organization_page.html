About the Organization

At Warm Threads Foundation, we are dedicated to bringing warmth and dignity to those in need by providing clothing donations to the homeless. Our mission is to alleviate the struggles faced by individuals experiencing homelessness by ensuring they have access to clean, weather-appropriate attire.

Founded on the principles of compassion and community support, we work tirelessly to collect, sort, and distribute clothing through a network of volunteers and partnerships with local shelters and outreach programs. By addressing a basic yet critical need, we aim to foster hope and empower our homeless neighbors to face their challenges with greater resilience. Join us in making a tangible difference in their lives, one garment at a time.
